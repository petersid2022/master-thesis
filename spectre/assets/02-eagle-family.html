Draft acceleration — a research trajectory

Eagle-family methods emphasize learned draft heads, feature reuse from the target, and tree / multi-candidate verification — useful mental map for your literature slide (names are thematic, not vendor-specific).

Generation I — standalone drafter

Separate small model or early-exit network proposes tokens; target verifies in parallel. Baseline speculative decoding story.

Generation II — draft sees target

Draft conditions on hidden states / attention from the large model so proposals stay aligned — fewer rejections, higher accept rate.

Generation III — trees & breadth

Multiple branches verified together; amortize target forward over a batch of hypotheses — throughput scales with hardware parallelism.
